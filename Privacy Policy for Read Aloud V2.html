Privacy Policy for Read Aloud V2

Effective Date: January 13, 2025

Read Aloud V2 ("we", "our", or "the extension") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Read Aloud V2 extension.

Data Collection and Usage

No Personal Data: Read Aloud V2 does not collect or store any personal information. The extension only processes the selected text from the webpage to convert it into speech using the browserâ€™s built-in speech synthesis (TTS) API.

Settings Storage: The extension stores basic settings such as speed, pitch, volume, and the selected voice model. These settings are used solely to provide a personalized user experience and are stored locally in your browser.

No External Data Access: The extension does not access, share, or transmit any data to external servers. All processing and data storage are local to your device.

Permissions

  • <all_urls>: This permission allows the extension to read and interact with content on any webpage. It is only used to retrieve and read aloud the selected text, and no personal or sensitive information is accessed or stored.
  • scripting and tts: These permissions are used to inject code into the webpage for DOM manipulation (e.g., for displaying the control player) and for text-to-speech functionality. No data other than the selected text is processed or stored.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third-party services. We do not use third-party analytics or ad networks.

Security

The extension does not collect or transmit sensitive user data, and all data processing is done locally within the browser. We ensure that no data is shared externally unless explicitly stated.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
